📖 SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT (LENGKAP)

Verb Agreement adalah aturan bahwa kata kerja harus sesuai dengan subjek dalam hal jumlah (singular/plural). Simak aturan utama:

1. Subjek Singular → verb + s/es
Ex: She works hard. The cat runs fast.
2. Subjek Plural → verb tanpa s
Ex: They play soccer. The dogs bark.
3. Compound subject with 'and' → plural
Ex: Tom and Jerry are friends.
4. Either/Or, Neither/Nor → verb ikuti subjek terdekat
Ex: Neither the teacher nor the students are ready.
5. Indefinite pronouns (everyone, each, someone) → singular
Ex: Everyone likes music.
6. Collective nouns (team, family) → singular/plural (makna kesatuan/individu)
Ex: The team is winning (kesatuan).
7. Expressions of quantity (a lot of, some) → tergantung objek
Ex: Some of the cake is left / Some cookies are gone.
8. Titles, distances, sums → singular
Ex: Ten dollars is enough. “The Hobbit” is a book.

Tips Penting: Jangan tertipu dengan frasa di antara subjek & verb. Fokus pada subjek utama!
